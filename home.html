About us
Coventry Health & Travel Clinic is an independent private run pharmacy
providing travel services and immunisations. We are pharmacist operated
in a family run clinic providing an alternative route of access to travel
jabs and immunisations in a residential surrounding away from busy town centres

We pride ourselves on our personal touch, flexible appointment availability and competitive prices


Our aim is to innovate and simply bring patients a completely different model of private pharmacy care providing overall better health care access.

Regulated Services

We provide services that are authorised by Patient Group Directions (PGDs) which are signed instructions from registered doctors. Our PGDs are provided by the Pharmadoctor and regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC).


Superintendent Pharmacist

Our services are provided by a registered pharmacists who are authorised to prescribe and administer medication by completing regular training and following patient group directions. Our superintendent pharmacist is Karandeep Briah (MPharm, MRPharmS).
